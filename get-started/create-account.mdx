To create an account on ZBD today and integrate global instant payments in all of your projects. Follow the quick steps below:
### Access ZBD Developer Dashboard

- Navigate to the [ZBD Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.zebedee.io/signup).

<Frame caption="ZBD Developer Dashboard | Sign Up">
  <img src="/img/v3/create-account.png" alt="ZBD Developer Dashboard | Sign Up" />
</Frame>
<br />

### Enter Your Information

- Fill in the form with your information, then press `Create Account` to register. Remember to meet all of the password requirements to proceed.
- An Invite Code is is currently required to create an account. If you don't have one, please contact your ZBD Customer Success Manager.
- You will also need to agree to the ZBD Terms of Service and Global Privacy Policy.

<Frame caption="ZBD Developer Dashboard | Sign Up">
  <img src="/img/v3/create-account-2.png" alt="ZBD Developer Dashboard | Sign Up" />
</Frame>
<br />

<Info>If you have an account on the ZBD Rewards app, you will need to use a **different email address** to create an account on the ZBD Developer Dashboard.</Info>  

### Verify Your Email

- After successful account creation, you should receive an email with a 6-digit verification code. Copy those digits and head on over to the dashboard to verify your email.

<Frame caption="ZBD Developer Dashboard | Verify Email">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup-verify-email.png" alt="ZBD Developer Dashboard | Verify Email" />
</Frame>
<br />

### You're set!

Now you can [complete your profile](/get-started/verify-identity) or begin making and receiving payments by [creating your first project](/get-started/create-project).
