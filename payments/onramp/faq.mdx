<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What currencies does ZBD Onramp support?">
    ZBD Onramp currently supports:
    - Bitcoin (BTC)
    - USD Coin (USDC)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What withdrawal methods are available?">
    Users can withdraw their funds to:
    - Bitcoin on-chain addresses
    - Lightning Network addresses
    - USDC addresses
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are currently supported?">
    Currently, ZBD Onramp supports:
    - Pay by Bank with ACH

    Coming soon:
    - Google Pay
    - Apple Pay
    - Credit Cards
    - Debit Cards
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What platforms and environments can I implement the widget on?">
    The ZBD Onramp widget is designed as an embeddable web view, making it compatible with virtually any development environment. The ZBD team is actively working on wrapper SDKs and libraries for popular development environments to make integration even easier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the pricing and fees?">
    For detailed pricing information and fee structures, please speak with your ZBD customer success manager or [connect with our sales team](https://zbd.one/sales).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is customer support handled?">
    Customer support is handled internally by the ZBD team. The ZBD Onramp widget includes a built-in chatbot that can handle a wide range of user inquiries, create support tickets directly, and even provide guides and detailed walkthroughs to help assist users.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What security measures are in place?">
    ZBD Onramp implements multiple layers of security:
    - Secure API endpoints
    - Encrypted data transmission and webhook payloads
    - Compliance with financial regulations (KYC, AML, etc.)
    - Real-time AI-based transaction monitoring and fraud detection
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the integration requirements?">
    To integrate ZBD Onramp, you need:
    1. A ZBD developer account
    2. API credentials
    3. A secure backend environment
    4. Compliance with our integration guidelines
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the transaction limits?">
    Transaction limits vary based on your specific needs. Contact your ZBD Customer Success Manager for specific limit details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
